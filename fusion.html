NUCLEAR FUSION

--------------------------------------------------------

In addition to my care for the environment, learning about nuclear fusion as potential energy source was instrumental in my choosing nuclear engineering as a major. In contrast to the convential means of extracting energy from nuclear interactions, fusion works by fusing two small atoms together to create a single, larger atom and excess energy. Notably, fusion is completely free of greenhouse gas emissions, safe, and efficient. I believe nuclear fusion can be the energy source of the future, and am excited to help get the technology become feasible. More information about fusion can be found on ITER's website.
